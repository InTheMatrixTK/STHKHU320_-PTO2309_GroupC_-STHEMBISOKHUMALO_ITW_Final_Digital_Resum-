Summary Of Experience

Mr Sthembiso Khumalo has a collective of 13 years’ experience in private and corporate companies. He is a registered Profession Associated Valuer with a wealth of property valuation knowledge and experience.

He has been responsible for undertaking an extensive range of property valuation and advisory services for purchase and sale, decision making, mortgage bond and development bond valuations, rental determinations, due diligence exercises, and shareholder reporting for JSE listed REITs in South Africa and in Sub Saharan Africa as well as other private and public clients.
